A Little About Myself...

My Socials

I was born and raised in Kerala, India, obtaining my Bachelors from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) in Madras. I went on to attend graduate school at Duke University, obtaining my Ph.D. in Statistics in 2008. After Duke, I was a Post Doctoral Research Fellow at the Center for Research in Statistical Methodology (CRiSM), University of Warwick, UK, during 2008-10.

I am now a Professor in the Department of Statistics, Harvard University.
